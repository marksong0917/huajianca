☀️ Sunroom

Transform your outdoor space into a year-round living area with our premium aluminum sunrooms. Engineered for Canadian weather, featuring high-strength frames, thermal breaks, and UV-protected glass. Enjoy natural light without sacrificing insulation — perfect for cozy winter gardens or summer lounges. Custom designs to match any home architecture.

🪟 Window Systems

Our aluminum windows combine sleek aesthetics with extreme durability. Multi-chamber thermal insulation, weather-resistant seals, and corrosion-proof frames ensure low maintenance for decades. Sliding, casement, or awning styles available — reduce energy bills while elevating your home's curb appeal.

🚿 Shower Partitions

Modernize your bathroom with frameless or semi-frameless aluminum shower enclosures. Engineered with premium tempered glass, anti-rust aluminum profiles, and smooth glide mechanisms. Easy to clean, minimalist design, and customizable dimensions — safety and elegance combined.

🚪 Doors (Entry & Patio)

Sophisticated aluminum entry doors, sliding patio doors, and folding systems. Heavy-duty hardware, multi-point locking, and powder-coated finishes available in any color. Security, thermal performance, and timeless beauty — from residential to commercial projects.

Huajian Aluminum Canada is a proud subgroup of Shandong HuaJian Aluminum Group, a global leader in aluminum manufacturing. HuaJian was founded in 2000 and has grown into a large enterprise with six production factories and an annual output of 800,000 tons of aluminum profiles . We bring world-class aluminum technology to the Canadian market. Our parent company operates full-process intelligent production lines, including a 10,000-ton extrusion press—one of the largest in the industry. Every product we offer is made in our advanced factories, where we control quality from raw material to finished goods.

We make all our products in-house. Nothing is ever outsourced. Our product range includes aluminum windows, doors, sunrooms, curtain walls, and shower partitions. Each one is built with industry-leading technology. Our products are trusted and sold in markets around the world. They have been used in many major global projects. Every door and window we produce is NAMI-certified and tested to meet CSA standards for the Canadian climate. This means reliable performance, even in harsh weather.
